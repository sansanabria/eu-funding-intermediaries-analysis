# EU Funding Decision Framework

## How to Use This Guide

This framework helps any company determine whether EU-backed funding applies to them, and if so, which financial intermediaries to approach. Follow the steps in order — each step narrows your shortlist.

At the end, you will have:
- A clear profile of what funding type fits your company
- A shortlist of fund managers to contact
- A direct filtered link to the EU portal

---

## Step 1 — Location Check

**Question:** Where does your company operate or where is your target market?

| Your Situation | Coverage |
|---|---|
| EU Member State (27 countries) | Full coverage |
| EEA country (Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein) | Covered |
| Switzerland, Turkey, UK | Partially covered |
| EU Outermost Region (Canary Islands, Madeira, Azores, Guadeloupe, Réunion, etc.) | Special dedicated programs available |
| Outside all of the above | EU intermediary funding likely not applicable |

> If you are in an **outermost region**, flag this — there are dedicated EU programs with additional support that go beyond the standard intermediary list.

---

## Step 2 — Company Category

**Question:** What best describes your company or project?

| Category | Description | Go to |
|---|---|---|
| Startup / Early-stage | Less than 3 years old, pre-revenue or early revenue | VC / Accelerator funds |
| SME | Under 250 employees, under €50M turnover | PE / Debt / VC funds |
| Mid-cap | 250–3,000 employees | PE / Infrastructure funds |
| Infrastructure project | Large-scale physical project (energy, water, transport) | Infrastructure / EIB funds |

> This maps directly to the **"Company Category"** filter on the EU portal.

---

## Step 3 — Type of Finance Needed

**Question:** What do you need funding for?

| You need... | Type of Finance | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Capital to build or expand | Loans / Debt | Private debt funds, bank-backed intermediaries |
| Equity investment, give up stake | Venture Capital / Private Equity | VC and PE fund managers |
| Very small amounts, social focus | Microfinance | Microfinance institutions |
| Project funding, no repayment | Grants & Contracts | EU grant programs (separate from this portal) |
| Flexible, hybrid structure | Hybrid debt-equity | Impact Bridge hybrid funds |

> This maps directly to the **"Type of Finance"** filter on the EU portal.

---

## Step 4 — Implementing Partner

**Question:** Which EU institution is backing the fund matters for eligibility and terms.

| Implementing Partner | Focus | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| EIF (European Investment Fund) | SMEs, VC, guarantees | Startups and SMEs needing growth capital |
| EIB (European Investment Bank) | Large infrastructure, long-term loans | Infrastructure projects €25M+ |
| InvestEU | Climate, digital, social, research | Broad sustainability and innovation projects |
| National Promotional Banks | Country-specific programs | Projects in a single country |
| Recovery & Resilience Facility | Green and digital transformation | Post-COVID recovery projects |

> This maps directly to the **"Implementing Partner"** filter on the EU portal.

---

## Step 5 — Funding Amount Range

**Question:** How much funding are you looking for?

| Amount Range | Likely Products | Relevant Intermediaries |
|---|---|---|
| Under €500K | Microfinance, small loans | Microfinance institutions |
| €500K – €5M | SME loans, small VC | Debt funds, early-stage VC |
| €5M – €20M | PE / Impact funds | Impact Bridge, OXY Capital |
| €20M – €50M | Infrastructure / Large PE | Suma Capital, NIAM, Alantra |
| €50M+ | EIB direct / large infrastructure | EIB direct lending |

> This maps directly to the **"Min/Max Amount"** filters on the EU portal.

---

## Step 6 — Sustainability & SDG Alignment

**Question:** Does your project or company address any of these areas?

| Your Focus Area | Relevant SDGs | Matching Fund Managers |
|---|---|---|
| Solar / Renewable Energy | SDG 7 | Suma Capital, Alantra, NIAM, Axon Partners |
| Green Hydrogen | SDG 7, SDG 13 | Suma Capital, Impact Bridge, Axon Partners |
| Desalination / Clean Water | SDG 6, SDG 14 | Impact Bridge (IB Impact Debt, IB Direct Debt) |
| Climate / Decarbonization | SDG 13 | Suma Capital, NIAM, Alantra |
| Digital Infrastructure | SDG 9 | Axon Partners, Alantra |
| Sustainable Agriculture | SDG 2 | Impact Bridge AgriFood Fund |
| Social / Financial Inclusion | SDG 1, SDG 10 | IB Impact Debt, Norrsken |
| None of the above | — | Commercial/generalist funds only |

> Impact-focused funds (Impact Bridge, Norrsken) **require SDG alignment** — they will evaluate your project against their impact measurement tools.

### Go to the EU Portal with Your Filters
Link: https://youreurope.europa.eu/business/finance-funding/getting-funding/access-finance/en/financial-intermediaries

Apply filters:
1. Select your country on the map
2. Set "Company Category"
3. Set "Type of Finance"
4. Set "Implementing Partner" (if known)
5. Set min/max amount range
6. Download your filtered results
